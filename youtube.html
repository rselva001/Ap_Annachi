About Our Rotary Club

Serving Tirunelveli with dedication since 1985

Our History

Chartered on June 15, 1985, the Rotary Club of Tamiraparani Tirunelveli has been a beacon of service in Southern Tamil Nadu. Named after the sacred Tamiraparani River, our club embodies the life-giving spirit of our region.

1985

Club Charter

Founded with 32 charter members under the leadership of our first President, Rtn. Muthuraman. Sponsored by Rotary Club of Tirunelveli.

1992

First Major Project

Established the Rotary Blood Bank in partnership with Tirunelveli Medical College, saving thousands of lives over three decades.

2005
<3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mb-2">Tsunami Relief

Mobilized ₹25 lakhs in aid and rebuilt 35 homes in coastal communities affected by the Indian Ocean tsunami.

2020-Present

Pandemic Response

Distributed PPE kits, oxygen concentrators, and vaccination support during COVID-19, serving over 50,000 community members.

Our Mission & Values

Guided by Rotary International's principles with local focus

Fellowship

Building lifelong friendships while serving our community, united by Rotary's ideal of "Service Above Self".

Service

Addressing Tirunelveli's most pressing needs through sustainable projects in health, education, and environment.

Global Network

Leveraging Rotary International's 1.4 million members across 200+ countries to create larger impact.

Part of Rotary International

As part of Rotary International District 3212, we're connected to a global network of 1.4 million neighbors, friends, and community leaders who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change.

Our club actively participates in Rotary's six areas of focus:

  • Peacebuilding
  • Disease Prevention
  • Water & Sanitation
  • Maternal & Child Health
  • Education
  • Economic Development
