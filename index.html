Our Recent Community Service

We recently organized a health camp in rural areas, providing free medical checkups and medicines to over 500 underprivileged individuals. Our team of volunteer doctors and healthcare professionals worked tirelessly to make this event a success.

This initiative is part of our ongoing commitment to improve healthcare access in underserved communities across the region.

Rotary Involvement

Active in various sectors including legal, engineering, and education. Originated the innovative "Lessons from Business Leaders" program that connects industry experts with young professionals. Spearheaded multiple donation drives providing essential supplies to hospitals and police stations during critical times.

Membership History

As Past District Governor (PDG), has made significant contributions to the Rotary community through visionary leadership. With over 15 years of dedicated service, has held various leadership positions and been instrumental in expanding Rotary's impact in the region.

Community Contributions

Led numerous community development initiatives including clean water projects, educational scholarships, and healthcare access programs. Organized annual health camps serving over 5,000 underprivileged community members. Established vocational training centers empowering local youth with employable skills.

Service Projects

Dedicated to creating global impact through service-driven programs. Current focus areas include polio eradication, literacy programs, and sustainable community development. Recently completed a major project providing solar lighting to 50 rural schools in the district.
