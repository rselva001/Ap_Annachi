Who We Are

The Rotary Club of Tamiraparani Tirunelveli is a dynamic group of professionals and community leaders dedicated to making a positive impact in our local community and around the world.

Founded in 1985, our club has been at the forefront of humanitarian service, fostering goodwill and peace through our commitment to improving health, supporting education, and alleviating poverty.

Member Spotlight

Meet our dedicated Rotarians who embody the spirit of "Service Above Self".

AP Annachi

Past President

AP Annachi has been an integral part of our Rotary family for over 25 years. His dedication to community service and leadership has inspired countless Rotarians and community members alike.

Under his presidency, our club initiated several landmark projects including the Tamiraparani River Restoration Project and the Rural Healthcare Mobile Units that continue to serve thousands of people annually.
